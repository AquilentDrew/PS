The task of enrolling tens of millions of uninsured people into health care products which they have little familiarity with or understanding of is a daunting task. The complex business and policy requirements deliver important benefits to people, but also impose more complexity on the process. Yet we will overcome these challenges by delivering a stellar user experience.

We will simplify wherever we can. We will hide complexity when it must exist. We will get people the information they need. We will engage with them in meaningful ways and build trust. We will move them through long processes with a guiding hand. As we learn more about our audience and their use of the website, will will quickly respond and improve.
