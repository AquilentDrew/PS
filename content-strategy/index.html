Content on healthcare.gov is designed to educate, engage, and drive users to enrollment. We accomplish this by presenting short, simple pages that provide details only when users need them. We use personalization to ensure the most useful related content is always displayed. We strictly enforce plain language and reading level. We integrate video and infographics, key to reaching lower-literacy audiences. The result is content that serves all users, works well on all platforms, and helps the uninsured understand their obligations, opportunities, and processes for getting health insurance.
