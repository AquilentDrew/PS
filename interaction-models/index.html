Healthcare.gov utilizes an Interaction model with three primary components: Header (with global and local aspects), Cards, and a Canvas (providing a ground for controls and negative space).

The regular use of these components creates a consistent experience that allows users to perform a broad range of different actions with a consistent experience; from somewhat passive activities such as consuming educational material to more active tasks such as applying for coverage.
